hanoko

- Hard work Hard change -

The 'reasonable' one of the group. She's notably more wealthy compared to the rest Hanako makes her money-- like the others- through bounty work, but also through street racing. Her pride and joy car is the highest valued thing she owns. blazing down the inter-past is one of the many ways she escapes from all the noise and chaos of the combusting city. Through her riches she collaborated with the company MOAB to be a first user/test subject of the cybernetic toughened skin she has, she has many other enhancements as well. Growing a bit of reputation within the company she's been made the "volunteered '' body guard of Jae Jae - the daughter of the company owners.
